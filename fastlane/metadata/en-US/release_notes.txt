Version 0.9.7 (Build 90021) brings significant improvements to workout logging and UI clarity. This update adds support for Reps-in-Reserve (RIR) with enhanced data propagation, introduces mathematical average fallback for rep ranges (e.g., "8-12"), and harmonizes diary summary cards for a cleaner look. We've also improved progress bar readability with dual-layer clipping for better text contrast and fixed various analytics layout issues.
