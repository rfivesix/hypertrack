This hotfix release brings important stability and functionality corrections to Train Libre:

* Fixed a tracking bug to ensure all logged supplements and their daily goal progress bars display correctly in the Diary and Supplement Hub.
* Fixed an analytics data calculation error that double-counted fluids on historical weight and nutrition charts.
* Fixed the automated backup framework to ensure data archives are safely created and stored in secure application paths.
* Fixed chart behaviors to make sure haptic feedback remains fully disabled when turned off in the app settings.
* Improved the exercise notes layout by adding direct tap-to-edit support and a clean delete function to manage your training reminders.
