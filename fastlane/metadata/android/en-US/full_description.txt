Train Libre is an open-source, offline-first fitness tracker for people who want serious training and nutrition logging without giving up control of their data.

Features:
- Log workouts set by set, including warm-ups, working sets, failure sets, dropsets, reps, weight, and RIR
- Build reusable routines and review completed sessions
- Track nutrition, meals, hydration, caffeine, creatine, supplements, bodyweight, and body measurements
- Use progress statistics for PRs, consistency, muscle volume, recovery, body trends, calories, sleep, steps, and pulse where available
- Keep app data local by default, with export and import for local backups
- Use integrated Open Food Facts and wger-based catalog sources
- Export supported app-recorded data one way to Google Health Connect
- Use optional AI meal features with your own API key, disabled by default

What makes Train Libre different:
- Open source and developed in public
- Offline-first, with no mandatory cloud account
- Built for structured gym tracking instead of gamification
- Transparent analytics designed to be understandable and useful over the long term

Train Libre is not a medical app. Recovery, sleep, and pulse features are intended for training guidance and personal tracking only and are not medical diagnosis or treatment.

Open Source:
The source code is publicly available and the app is developed in the open.
