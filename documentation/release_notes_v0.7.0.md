# Hypertrack v0.7.0 — Release Notes

Hypertrack v0.7.0 marks the transition from alpha builds to a regular minor release, with a major step forward in analytics depth, product stability, and day-to-day usability.

## 🎉 New Features

- **Statistics & Analytics Hub is now fully integrated:** Get a central overview of consistency, PR progress, muscle distribution, recovery readiness, and body/nutrition trends.
- **Deep-dive analytics screens:** Jump from hub summaries into focused dashboards for PRs, consistency tracking, recovery analysis, muscle-group trends, and body/nutrition correlation.
- **Universal sharing workflows:** You can now export and share app-generated content (including text summaries and exported files) through native OS share sheets for faster collaboration or coach feedback.

## 🚀 Improvements

- **Smarter analytics architecture:** Statistics now use clearer feature boundaries (domain/data/presentation), making analytics behavior more consistent and maintainable.
- **More reliable range logic:** Time-range handling is standardized across hub and drill-down views, so metrics remain easier to interpret.
- **Improved analytics readability:** Labels, chart defaults, and numeric formatting have been unified across statistics screens for cleaner trend reading.
- **Data-quality-aware insights:** Body/nutrition and muscle analytics now apply clearer confidence and sufficiency rules before presenting stronger guidance.

## 🐛 Fixes & Stability

- **Refined statistics behavior:** Several v0.7 alpha rough edges in analytics state handling and presentation consistency were addressed.
- **Core tracking polish:** Workout and nutrition logging received targeted reliability and UX refinements during the v0.7 stabilization cycle.

## 🔐 Privacy & Control

- **Offline-first analytics remains intact:** All insights are still computed on-device from your existing logs.
- **No cloud dependency required:** Hypertrack continues to prioritize local data ownership and privacy by default.
